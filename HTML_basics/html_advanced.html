Why are turtles so slow

Turtles are generally slow due to a combination of factors, including their herbivorous diet, protective shells, and the lack of strong evolutionary pressures to be fast. They don't need to chase food or run from predators, and their shells provide ample defense, making speed less crucial for survival.
